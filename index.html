Finn Beruldsen

Welcome

I am a Doctoral researcher at the Center for Nuclear Receptors and Cell Signaling (CNRCS) at the Univeristy of Houston. I specialize in T-cell receptor biology, examining it through both computational and structural lenses. Under the guidance of Dr. Dinler Antunes, I develop software designed to enhance cell-based immunotherapies by improving their targeting, sensitivity, and safety.

Things I Can Do

  • Analyze biological data (Python, R, NAMD, Gromacs)
  • Develop bioinformatics pipelines
  • Apply machine learning to immunotherapy research
  • Perform experimental immunology research
